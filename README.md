Struckmire:
  Struckmire will be a top down survival adventure game where you need to hunt for resources, explore the island, and make your decision.
  Escape... or Stay...
  You wake up on shore with a forest near you. There will be 3 Biomes (forest, desert, and tundra) along with 2 structures (village,
  and goblin outpost). There will be many mobs, friendly and unfriendly.
  Figure out the rest yourself.
  Have Fun!
  TLDR: Similar to muck but with more biomes, progression, less bosses.
